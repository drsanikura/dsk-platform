# DSK Platform Design System

Version: 1.0

Date: 19 July 2026

Owner: Dr. Sani Kura

---

# 1. Brand Identity

## Brand Name

Dr. Sani Kura

## Brand Purpose

To make trustworthy, evidence-based health knowledge accessible to individuals, families and communities.

## Brand Personality

The platform should feel:

- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Professional
- Compassionate
- Educational
- Modern

The user should feel:

"I can trust this information."

---

# 2. Design Philosophy

The DSK Platform follows these principles:

## Clarity over complexity

Medical information should be easy to understand.

## Trust over excitement

The design should feel like a trusted healthcare professional, not entertainment media.

## Human over technical

Technology should disappear behind a simple experience.

# 7. Components

## Buttons

Primary Button:

Used for important actions.

Examples:

- Explore Health Library
- Read Article


Style:

- Emerald background
- Dark text
- Rounded


Secondary Button:

Used for less important actions.

Style:

- Transparent
- Border
- Navy text


---

## Cards

Used for:

- Articles
- Videos
- Health topics
- Features


Style:

- White background
- Soft shadow
- Rounded corners


---

# 8. Layout

Maximum content width:

1280px


Desktop:

Large comfortable reading areas.


Mobile:

Everything must remain usable with one hand.

---

# 9. Accessibility

The platform must support:

- Clear contrast
- Keyboard navigation
- Readable font sizes
- Simple language

---

# 10. Component Naming Rules

Components use PascalCase.

Examples:

Good:

ArticleCard.tsx

VideoCard.tsx


Bad:

health-card.tsx

card1.tsx
