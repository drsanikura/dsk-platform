# DSK Platform – Product Vision

Version: 1.0

Date: 19 July 2026

Owner: Dr. Abubakar Muhammad Sani

---

# Mission

To empower individuals, families, and communities with trustworthy, evidence-based medical knowledge through accessible digital technology.

---

# Vision

To become Africa's most trusted digital health platform by providing reliable medical education, preventive healthcare information, and innovative digital health solutions.

---

# Core Values

- Trust
- Compassion
- Evidence-Based Medicine
- Simplicity
- Accessibility
- Innovation
- Privacy

---

# Target Audience

### Primary

- Patients
- Parents
- Students
- Families

### Secondary

- Healthcare professionals
- Medical students
- Hospitals
- NGOs
- Government agencies

---

# Version 1 Objectives

The first release should allow visitors to:

- Learn about Dr. Sani Kura
- Read health articles
- Search medical topics
- Watch educational videos
- Contact the team

---

# Future Objectives

- AI Health Assistant
- Symptom Guidance
- Health Calculators
- Online Courses
- Community Forum
- Telemedicine
- Hospital Management Integration

---

# Success Principles

Every feature added to the platform must satisfy at least one of these goals:

- Improve health education
- Increase accessibility
- Build trust
- Simplify healthcare information
- Help users make informed health decisions

---

# Engineering Philosophy

- Build small.
- Test often.
- Keep it simple.
- Document decisions.
- Never sacrifice quality for speed.
